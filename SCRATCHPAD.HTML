General Description

The Cornerstone is a prestigious new development in the heart of Stillorgan Village, a lively suburban neighbourhood just a 20-minute drive from Stephens Green. This location offers the best of both worlds: the charm of village living with the city, coast, and mountains all within easy reach, thanks to excellent transportation options. You'll find an array of shops, eateries, and leisure spots right at your doorstep.

Experience top-tier urban living with exclusive amenities including a gym, communal kitchen and lounge area, games room, rooftop terrace, and a state-of-the-art business centre. The landscaped internal courtyard features a private children's play area and benches for relaxation.

Space

At The Cornerstone, you'll discover a variety of luxury apartments designed for modern living, each featuring a balcony to relax and take in the views. These meticulously crafted apartments are filled with natural light and boast high-end finishes and thoughtful details throughout ensuring a truly inviting living experience.

Neighbourhood

The apartments are in the heart of Stillorgan village which has numerous pubs, restaurants, provide plenty of options to explore. With its convenient local amenities and strong sense of community, The Cornerstone offers an ideal lifestyle in the heart of a vibrant village.

Transit

Getting to The Cornerstone couldn't be easier. We are right on the N11 with a Quality Bus Corridor which means you can be in the City Centre in less than 30 minutes. Aircoach provides a direct link to Dublin Airport via the city centre. There is ample basement parking (nightly fee of €10 applies) and a place to park your bike or E Bike or E-Scooter. Blackrock Village, Dun Laoghaire seafront and Dundrum Town Centre are all less than 20-minute drive.
